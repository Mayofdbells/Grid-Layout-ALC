Welcome to Vickies Toys!

Do you know that toys are essential to the growth of a child?
They are actually the first friends children keep, so your choice of toys for your child will go a long way in forming the intellectual capability of your child.

Although, some people are of the opinion that toys make children play in isolation. That is , it makes it difficult for them to play with their peers especially when they often let alone with the toys. Still, one cannot overrate the fact that whatever object a child plays with,is a toy to him at that moment.

So what do I do?

Everyone wants his or her child to grow into a brilliant antelligent child but this cannot be achieved alone by the genes of the parent. Hence; at Vickies Toys, we guide you on the right choices of toys tomake for your child so as to ensure that your child grows into that intelligent gentleman or lady.
